About Me

In my current role as a Front End Software Developer at Hanna Andersson, I’ve had the opportunity to hone many skills quickly. I am very talented with CSS/SASS, have passion for semantic HTML, and always strive to implement the most logical and maintainable solutions for complicated business needs. I focus on writing performant and scalable JS and have experience with debugging and testing my code. I’m also very familiar with debugging legacy code and tech such as jQuery and older versions of vanilla JS. In addition to efficient and intuitive coding skills, I bring my experience from previous careers to the table! I’ve found that many of the experiences I’ve had over my 8 years as a retail manager, 4 years as a cosmetologist, and 10+ years as a drummer are very useful as a software developer. I believe these skills help me to be a well rounded partner for my team.

Aside from work, I enjoy nerding out with my coding mentor every week by recreating other technologies, learning new tech, and building apps. I’m also enjoying the process of revamping my nonprofits website in my spare time. It was originally built with Google Sites and is not very dazzling, reflective of my skills, nor responsive so it must be reborn!

I’m in love with learning new languages (coding and spoken). I’m fluent in English, advanced with French, understand a good amount of Italian and Spanish, and am a beginner with German and Japanese.

I’m open to new opportunities, especially those working with technologies such as React and Next.JS!

I'm a person that loves fun times, cats, and adventures with friends. In the beginning of 2021, I helped found a nonprofit in my hometown to support and mentor at-risk youth. Our name is Anaheim Youtopia (AY)! Currently, I'm the Exectuive Director and Treasurer of AY.

That's my face!
